Championship Rules

Race Sessions

Sessions are on Monday evenings starting at 9pm.

We will complete at least one race in a session but can continue until the first person decides to withdraw for the evening. All subsequent races that evening will be non-championship events.

Championship Race Points Awarded

  1. 10 points
  2.  8 points
  3.  6 points
  4.  4 points
  5.  2 points
  6.  1 point

Aggressive Driving

Excessive aggressive driving will be reported to the race steward (‘Xbox Record That’) for review with possible disqualification or championship points deducted.

Penalty Points

Every driver starts with a clean license at the beginning of a championship.

Penalty points are then awarded for reckless driving or excessive contact between cars. More than 1 penalty point can be given for serious incidents dependant on the Race Stewards ruling.

1 penalty point on a license = 2 championship points deducted another penalty point on a license = an extra 4 championship points deducted Each additional penalty point on a license = double the previous championship points deducted

So 5 penalty points on a license is –32 championship points.

Warnings can be given out which would amount to 0.5 a penalty point. Only whole penalty points equate to championship point deductions.

Game Settings

Damage will be on Simulation.

Assisted braking and Racing line will be turned off.
