Championship Rules

Race Sessions

Sessions are on Monday evenings starting at 9pm.

We will complete at least one race in a session but can continue until the first person decides to withdraw for the evening. All subsequent races that evening will be non-championship events.

Championship Race Points Awarded

  1. 10 points
  2.  8 points
  3.  6 points
  4.  4 points
  5.  2 points
  6.  1 point

Aggressive Driving and Penalties

Excessive aggressive driving will be reported to the race steward (‘Xbox Record That’) for review with possible disqualification or championship points deducted. The race steward may request a live stream from each driver via Twitch (‘Xbox Broadcast’).

Game Settings

Damage will be on Simulation.

Assisted braking is agreed to be turned off, but this is based on sportsmanship as there is no real way of telling.

Racing line should be turned off.
